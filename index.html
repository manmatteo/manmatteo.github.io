Matteo Manighetti

About me

I am a former research assistant at the University of Bologna, working with Claudio Sacerdoti Coen. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science in 2023 from the Institut Polytechnique de Paris, where my advisor has been Dale Miller.

My interests are (in a somehow decreasing order of generality): proof theory, proof checking and proof assistants, intuitionism, proof-theoretic semantics, semi-classical logics, focusing, admissible rules of proof systems, effects in functional languages.
